About JSTARC

Jalnawala's Sports Training and Research Center

JSTARC has become synonymous with major activities in martial art in India today. Better known for its innovative programs in Taekwondo & Hapkido, JSTARC was formed with an intention of promoting various fitness & sports related programs.

Official Kukkiwon Representative
